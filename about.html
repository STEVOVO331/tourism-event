Histoty

The maletsunyane braai festival was created to promote tourism in semonkong. The event attracts visitors from Lesotho and neighbouring countries to enjoy food, music, and outdoor activities

significance

The festival helps to promote local culture and suppports the local economy by creating opportunities for farmers, vendors, and tourism businesses
