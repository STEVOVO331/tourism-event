Welcome

The maletsunyane braai festival is one of the most exciting tourism events in Lesotho.It celebrates food, music, and basotho culture while showcasing the beauty of seemonkong, and the famous maletsunyane falls.

Event Details

  • Location:Semonkong, Lesotho
  • Venue:Near maletsunyane falls
  • Date:28 November
  • Activities:braai, music, adventure tourism
